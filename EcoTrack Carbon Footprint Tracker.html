üåø Reduce Your Carbon Footprint

  • Transportation: Carpool, use public transit, cycle, or walk when possible. Electric vehicles reduce emissions by 50%.
  • Energy: Switch to renewable energy, use LED bulbs, improve insulation, and unplug devices when not in use.
  • Food: Reduce meat consumption, buy local produce, minimize food waste, and compost organic matter.
  • Water: Take shorter showers, fix leaks, install water-saving fixtures, and collect rainwater for plants.
